EE Canvas v50 updater

This updater adds a mouse-resizable Text + Math side panel to your v49 HTML. It works entirely in your browser and does not upload your file.

Choose ee_canvas_homework_v49_toggle_text_math_panel.html.
  • Drag the panel's left edge to change its width.
  • Double-click the left edge to reset to 372 px.
  • The width is saved locally and restored when the app opens again.
  • Minimum width: 280 px. Maximum width: 700 px or 65% of the browser width.
